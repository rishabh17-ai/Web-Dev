Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India, and passed away on July 27, 2015. Kalam is widely regarded as one of India's most influential scientists and a beloved figure in Indian society.

Achievements

  • Played a key role in India's civilian space program and military missile development.
  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle(SLV-3).
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Wrote books like"Wings of Fire " and "India 2020".
  • Received the Bharat Ratna , India's highest civilian award.
